## 3. Keyboard-Specific Content Behavior

The Memeboard Keyboard processes input as follows:

- **Search within Memeboard's meme search field**: when you actively type in the Memeboard search field, your query is sent to our backend to retrieve relevant memes.
- **GIF search and browse**: when you use the GIF tab, search queries are sent to our GIF content provider (Klipy) to fetch results.
- **Typing in other apps**: the Memeboard Keyboard does not log, store, transmit, or analyze text you type in fields outside Memeboard's own search.

We do not capture, store, or transmit:

- Passwords
- Credit card numbers
- Sensitive financial information
- Personal messages
- Search queries entered into other apps (Google, YouTube, banking apps, messaging apps, etc.)

## 5. Fair Use, Fair Dealing, and Transformative Use Notice

We curate and distribute memes under the following principles:

- **United States**: Section 107 of the U.S. Copyright Act recognizes fair use for purposes including criticism, comment, news reporting, teaching, scholarship, and parody. We rely on fair use to share short transformative excerpts.
- **United Kingdom and EU**: Fair dealing for purposes of caricature, parody, or pastiche (UK CDPA 1988 §30A; EU Copyright Directive Article 17(7)) protects transformative meme content.
- **India**: Section 52 of the Indian Copyright Act exempts fair dealing including criticism and review.
- **United Arab Emirates**: Federal Law No. 38 of 2021 recognizes fair use for educational and informational purposes within reasonable limits.
- **Other jurisdictions**: Similar fair-use, fair-dealing, or quotation doctrines apply in most major copyright frameworks.

We strive to use only what is necessary, transform the source, and avoid commercial exploitation that would substitute for the original work.

## 9. Copyright Complaints and Takedown Procedure

We respond to copyright complaints in accordance with applicable law. If you believe content in Memeboard infringes your copyright, please send us a takedown notice with the following information:

### 9.1 Required information

To process a copyright complaint, please include:

1. **Identification of the copyrighted work** you claim has been infringed (title, original publication, URL or other reference)
2. **Identification of the allegedly infringing content** in Memeboard — describe the meme, the category or context where it appears, and any meme identifier visible in the App if available
3. **Your contact information** — full legal name, mailing address, phone number, and email
4. **A good-faith statement** that you believe the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
5. **A statement under penalty of perjury** that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on the owner's behalf
6. **Your physical or electronic signature**

Send the notice to: [samwisestudios01@gmail.com](mailto:samwisestudios01@gmail.com)

### 9.2 Response timeline

We aim to review valid takedown notices and act within **7 business days** of receipt. Where the complaint is clear and well-supported, we generally remove or restrict access to the disputed content faster than this commitment.

### 9.3 Counter-notification procedure

If your content was removed and you believe it was removed in error or as a result of misidentification, you may submit a counter-notification including:

1. **Identification of the removed content** and where it appeared before removal
2. **A statement under penalty of perjury** that you have a good-faith belief the content was removed by mistake or misidentification
3. **Your contact information** — full legal name, mailing address, phone number, and email
4. **A statement consenting to jurisdiction** in the courts of Dubai, UAE
5. **Your physical or electronic signature**

Send counter-notifications to: [samwisestudios01@gmail.com](mailto:samwisestudios01@gmail.com)

If we receive a valid counter-notification, we may restore the content unless the original complainant files legal action within a reasonable period.

### 9.4 Repeat infringers

We may suspend or terminate accounts or contributors who repeatedly upload infringing content.

### 9.5 Designated agent (forthcoming)

For United States Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) compliance, we are in the process of registering a designated agent with the U.S. Copyright Office. Until that registration is complete, all copyright complaints should be sent to the email address above and will be processed under our internal takedown procedure described in this section.
