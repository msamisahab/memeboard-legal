# Account & Data Deletion — Memeboard

**Developer:** Samwise & Co.
**App:** Memeboard

## How to delete your account

You can delete your Memeboard account and all associated data directly in the app:

1. Open the Memeboard app
2. Open the navigation **drawer** (menu)
3. Tap **Account**
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Confirm in the dialog

Your account and data are deleted immediately.

## What is deleted

Upon deletion, we permanently and immediately remove:
- Your profile information (name and email from Google Sign-In)
- Your saved favorites
- Your send history
- Your meme interaction signals

## What is retained

- A one-way SHA-256 hash of your account identifier is retained solely for legal compliance records (GDPR Article 30). This cannot be reversed to identify you.
- Aggregate, anonymized usage analytics containing no personal identifiers.
